# Repository Governance & AI Ethics Policy

## 1. Overview
This repository operates under a "Human-in-the-Loop" (HITL) model. While we leverage AI for research and drafting, all final commits are reviewed by a human legal editor to ensure compliance with the **BNS 2023** and **IT Rules 2025**.

## 2. AI Attribution & SGI Traceability
Per MeitY Rule 3(3), all content in this repo generated by AI is marked as **Synthetically Generated Information (SGI)**.
* **Model Engine:** [Gemini, Gemma]
* **Provenance:** Every SGI file contains a metadata stamp in the footer for traceability.
* **Verification:** Case law and statutory citations are manually verified against primary sources.

## 3. Contributor License Agreement (CLA) for AI-Assisted Code
By contributing to this repository, you agree to the following **AI-CLA**:
* **Ownership:** You represent that you have the right to contribute the code/text, even if AI-assisted.
* **Disclosure:** Contributors MUST label any PR that contains more than 20% AI-generated content with the `SGI-Contribution` tag.
* **Non-Infringement:** You certify that the AI tool used was not prompted to reproduce copyrighted code or licensed legal templates without authorization.

## 4. Grievance Redressal Mechanism
We take "AI Hallucinations" seriously. 
* **Officer:** [Your Name/Handle]
* **Process:** Please use our [AI Error Report Template](link-to-issue-template) to report inaccuracies.
* **SLA:** We acknowledge reports within 24 hours and aim for resolution within 15 days.

## 5. Ethical Guardrails
We do not permit the use of this repo's data for:
1. Training models that promote "Social Scoring."
2. Generating non-consensual deepfakes.
3. Automated legal decision-making without human oversight.
